Automobili Lamborghini is an Italian luxury supercar manufacturer founded in 1963 in Sant’Agata Bolognese, Italy. The brand was born out of engineering pride and a refusal to accept mediocrity.

Lamborghini reshaped the automotive world with legendary models like the Miura and Countach, introducing radical design, mid-engine layouts, and emotional performance that defined the modern supercar.

Today, Lamborghini blends Italian drama with modern engineering, creating cars that are loud, aggressive, and unapologetically bold in both design and performance.
